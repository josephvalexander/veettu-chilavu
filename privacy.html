Privacy Policy

Veettu Chilavu · Last updated: April 2026

What this app is

Veettu Chilavu ("വീട്ടു ചിലവ്") is a free, open-source family expense tracker. It runs entirely in your browser and stores all data in a Google Sheet that you own.

Data we access

When you sign in with Google, this app requests access to:

  • Google Sheets — to read and write expense data to a specific spreadsheet you provide. We only access the sheet you explicitly connect.
  • Your Google profile (name, email, profile picture) — displayed inside the app to identify who added an expense.

Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not store any data on our servers — there are no servers.
  • We do not share your data with any third party.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or analytics beyond basic page-view counts.
  • We do not store your Google credentials. OAuth tokens are held only in your browser's memory for the current session.

Your Google Sheet

All expense data is written directly to a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive. You have full ownership and control. You can delete or revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

Revoking access

You can revoke this app's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing "Veettu Chilavu".
